# Subscription And Streak Rules

## Subscription Rules

- The paid core app remains usable offline without an online subscription.
- Online sync, study buddies, messaging, and friend streaks require a signed-in account plus an active or grace-period subscription.
- Expired subscriptions remove online premium capability but do not delete owned offline decks or notes.

## Streak Rules

- A streak is built from explicit qualifying study activity events.
- Events are normalized by calendar day before counting.
- Duplicate study events on the same day should not inflate the streak.
- Buddy-linked streaks are tracked separately from solo streaks.
- Near-term support and analytics should be able to audit why a streak value exists from the event history.

## UX Rules

- The app should show current streak posture without implying content loss when a subscription lapses.
- Users without a subscription can still see that online features exist, but offline study remains first-class.
